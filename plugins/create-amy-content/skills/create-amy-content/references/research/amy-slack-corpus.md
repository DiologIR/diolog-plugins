## Observed mechanics (for the chat variant)

- **Openers:** "Goood morning" (stretch-spelled), "Morning!", "Hey", "Hello hope you slept well?", "FYI" for updates. No formal greetings mid-thread.
- **Abbreviations in-voice:** "nws", "No presh", "potensh", "bc", "pre cute", "2:30/3ish", "tee'd up".
- **Bullets (•) for any multi-item update**; bold via *asterisks* for the key line.
- **Exclamation-dense but genuine**; doubles ("!!") when delighted; "haha" as a softener inside otherwise-direct product critique.
- **Typos and lowercase survive** ("havent", "dont", "exaclty", lowercase "i") - she types fast and doesn't polish DMs. A perfectly-polished Slack message would be out of voice.
- **Questions carry the work**: most substantive messages end in a real question or a "could you please…" with "No rush but…" softening.
- **Candour about her own state** (unwell, overwhelmed, blocked) delivered plainly, paired with a plan; mild swearing appears in this private register ("holy shit", "a bit of a shit day") - never in anything external.
- **Numbers stay exact** in wins ($ figures, dates, counts).
