## What the posts do (observed patterns)

- **Topics:** IR practice and its unsolved questions, AI-in-IR adoption (with sourced stats), Diolog milestones and investor updates, startup life with self-deprecating honesty, event takeaways.
- **Hooks:** a pointed question naming a real IR tension, a stat that raises an eyebrow, or a dry one-liner ("Buy now, have your shares sold by Zip later...").
- **Diolog appears as context, not pitch** - "A lot of these questions shape how we innovate at Diolog."
- **Genuine asks:** she requests feedback and introductions plainly and often, and means it.
- **Playful stretch-spelling and emphasis in-voice:** "waaaaay harder", "(!!!)", the ":(" emoticon. Humour is dry and often at her own expense (the escape room; "should I be worried?").
- **Data cited with source and precise numbers** (Nasdaq report percentages), then interpreted - the stat is never left to speak for itself.
